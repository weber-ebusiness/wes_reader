What does it do?
----------------

This extension integrates an online screen reader into your TYPO3 website. It creates a link opening
a small HTML5 audio player either globally via TypoScript or on specified pages only.

wes_reader uses Linguatec Voice Reader Web.
It is the official extension, which was developed by Weber eBusiness Services on behalf of Linguatec.
